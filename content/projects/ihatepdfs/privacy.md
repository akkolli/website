I Hate PDFs does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app works with local PDF files on your Mac. It does not upload documents, annotations, comments, filenames, reading activity, device identifiers, analytics, diagnostics, or usage data to Akshay Kolli or any server operated for I Hate PDFs.

I Hate PDFs does not use tracking, advertising, user accounts, or third-party analytics.

Any PDF content or annotations you create remain on your device unless you choose to share those files yourself outside the app.

If this policy changes, the updated policy will be posted on this page.
